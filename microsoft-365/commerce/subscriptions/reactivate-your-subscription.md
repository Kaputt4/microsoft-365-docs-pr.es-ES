      - <span data-ttu-id="0fb6a-114">Si su suscripción ha expirado, después de enviar los detalles de pago, la suscripción vuelve a un estado activo y la siguiente fecha de **facturación** se extiende un mes o un año, en función de su compromiso de suscripción actual.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fb6a-114">If your subscription was expired, then after you submit your payment details, your subscription returns to an active state, and the **Next billing** date extends by either one month or one year, depending on your current subscription commitment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fb6a-115">Si paga con tarjeta de crédito o cuenta bancaria, se le cobrará la extensión a su tarjeta de crédito o cuenta bancaria.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fb6a-115">If you pay by credit card or bank account, your credit card or bank account will be charged for the extension.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fb6a-116">Si paga mediante factura, verá la extensión reflejada en la siguiente factura.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fb6a-116">If you pay by invoice, you'll see the extension reflected on your next invoice.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0fb6a-117">Para asegurarse de que la suscripción no expira de nuevo, [Active facturación recurrente](renew-your-subscription.md#turn-recurring-billing-off-or-on).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fb6a-117">To make sure that your subscription doesn't expire again, [turn Recurring billing on](renew-your-subscription.md#turn-recurring-billing-off-or-on).</span></span>

    > <span data-ttu-id="0fb6a-118">La capacidad para pagar por cuenta bancaria no está disponible en algunos países o regiones.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0fb6a-118">The ability to pay by bank account isn't available in some countries or regions.</span></span>
